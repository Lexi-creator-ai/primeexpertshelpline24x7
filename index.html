Steps to Activate Prime Video on Your TV

Follow these official steps to activate Amazon Prime Video on your smart TV or streaming device. If you need assistance at any point, call our helpline at +1-888-766-1625.

  1. Download and install the Prime Video app from your device's app store (e.g., on your smart TV, Roku, Fire TV, etc.).
  2. Open the Prime Video app on your device.
  3. Select "Sign in and start watching" if you want to enter your Amazon account details directly on the device (not recommended for TVs with poor keyboards).
  4. Alternatively, choose "Register on the Amazon website" to receive a 5-6 character activation code displayed on your screen.
  5. On a separate device (like a computer or smartphone), go to the official Amazon website: amazon.com/code.
  6. Sign in to your Amazon account.
  7. Enter the activation code shown on your TV and select "Register Device."
  8. Your device should now be activated, and you can start streaming. If you face any errors, such as code not working or login issues, contact us for support.

Note: Always use the official Amazon website for entering codes to ensure security. We do not handle code entry; our support is via phone guidance only.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Amazon Prime Video?
Amazon Prime Video is a streaming service offered by Amazon that provides access to movies, TV shows, and original content as part of an Amazon Prime membership.
Why is my activation code not working?
Common reasons include typing errors, expired codes (they last about 30 minutes), or network issues. Try generating a new code or ensure you're signed into the correct Amazon account. Call us for step-by-step troubleshooting.
How do I fix buffering or streaming issues on Prime Video?
Check your internet connection, restart your device, clear the app cache, or update the app. If problems persist, our experts can help diagnose hardware or account-specific issues.
Is this service free?

Can I cancel my Prime membership through this site?
No, we are independent. Please manage your membership directly on the official Amazon website.
